  1. Question

    A standard eight-sided die was rolled 100 times, and the results were organized into the pie chart shown below.



    1. Evaluate #[𝐱<3.5]\#[\mathbf{x}<3.5].
    2. Evaluate #[𝐱>6.5]\#[\mathbf{x}>6.5].
    3. Evaluate #[|𝐱−4.5|<2]\#[|\mathbf{x}-4.5|<2].
    4. Evaluate #[|𝐱−5.5|>1]\#[|\mathbf{x}-5.5|>1].
    5. Evaluate half-integer boundary bb such that #[𝐱<b]=43\#[\mathbf{x}<b] = 43.
    6. Evaluate half-integer boundary bb such that #[𝐱>b]=70\#[\mathbf{x}>b] = 70.
    7. Evaluate half-integer radius rr such that #[|𝐱−7|<r]=9\#[|\mathbf{x}-7|<r] = 9.
    8. Evaluate half-integer radius rr such that #[|𝐱−7|>r]=91\#[|\mathbf{x}-7|>r] = 91.

    Solution

    The first 4 questions involve adding up frequencies of the indicated intervals. The last 4 questions can be done by guessing and checking until something works.


    1. There are 43 measurements less than 3.5. So, #[𝐱<3.5]=43\#[\mathbf{x}<3.5]=43.
    2. There are 25 measurements more than 6.5. So, #[𝐱>6.5]=25\#[\mathbf{x}>6.5]=25.
    3. There are 45 measurements closer than 2 units from 4.5. So, #[|𝐱−4.5|<2]=45\#[|\mathbf{x}-4.5|<2]=45.
    4. There are 81 measurements further than 1 units from 5.5. So, #[|𝐱−5.5|>1]=81\#[|\mathbf{x}-5.5|>1]=81.
    5. A boundary at b=3.5b=3.5 has 43 measurements less than it. So, #[𝐱<3.5]=43\#[\mathbf{x}<3.5]=43.
    6. A boundary at b=2.5b=2.5 has 70 measurements more than it. So, #[𝐱>2.5]=70\#[\mathbf{x}>2.5]=70.
    7. An interval with radius r=0.5r=0.5 around center 7 contains 9 measurements. So, #[|𝐱−7|<0.5]=9\#[|\mathbf{x}-7|<0.5]=9.
    8. An interval with radius r=0.5r=0.5 around center 7 excludes 91 measurements. So, #[|𝐱−7|>0.5]=91\#[|\mathbf{x}-7|>0.5]=91.
