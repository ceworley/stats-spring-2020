make_frequency_distribution

  1. Question

    A random procedure generated many measurements: download data

    Please complete the frequency distribution using breaks 20, 25, 30, 35, 40, 45, 50:

    Interval Frequency
    20 to 25
    25 to 30
    30 to 35
    35 to 40
    40 to 45
    45 to 50


    Solution

    You will want to use a computer to answer these questions.

    In a spreadsheet, open the data, add the breaks as a column; then, use the FREQUENCY function.

    In R, open the data and use the hist function. You supply the breaks and read the counts:

    mydata = read.csv("make_freq_dist.csv")
x = mydata$x
myhist = hist(x,breaks=c(20,25,30,35,40,45,50))
    myhist$counts
    ## [1]  4  6  4 15 25 66
    interval frequency
    20 to 25 4
    25 to 30 6
    30 to 35 4
    35 to 40 15
    40 to 45 25
    45 to 50 66
