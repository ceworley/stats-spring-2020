    Solution

    To determine the range by hand, you subtract the minimum value from the maximum value. You could first sort all the data. sort(𝐱)=46.11,46.60,46.69,46.86,47.02,47.21,47.46,47.49,47.80,47.81,48.08,48.10,48.12,48.36,48.47\text{sort}(\mathbf{x}) = 46.11, 46.60, 46.69, 46.86, 47.02, 47.21, 47.46, 47.49, 47.80, 47.81, 48.08, 48.10, 48.12, 48.36, 48.47 Determine the minimum. min(𝐱)=46.11\text{min}(\mathbf{x}) = 46.11 Determine the maximum. max(𝐱)=48.47\text{max}(\mathbf{x}) = 48.47 Take the difference. range(𝐱)=max(𝐱)−min(𝐱)=48.47−46.11=2.36\text{range}(\mathbf{x}) = \text{max}(\mathbf{x})-\text{min}(\mathbf{x}) = 48.47-46.11 = 2.36
